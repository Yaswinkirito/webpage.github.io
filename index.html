Welcome to The Future

We all dream of the future flying cars, large awe-inspiring buildings, jetpacks, space vacations are some of the images that into pop our mind. Most of them we owe to the science fiction films and novels that we grew up with, but it has been a question that plagued us since time immemorial. In the 20th writers with their incredible imagination have written stories about how the 21st century will look like but what it really looked like was beyond any person thought at that time, if you asked your parents and grandparents if they ever imagined if the present-day tech was possible most of them would say “No”.

During the last few decades since the dawn of the information age the technological growth have been mind boggling and even now new innovations are being made day by day which sometimes makes us reconsider our common sense till then. Here I would like to introduce to the reader some of the newer innovations that occurred during the last few decades. Plant based tech such as cloud storage, Grown furniture, Bioluminescent trees, Super conducting diode, Cultured meat, Civilian Space Vacations industries and Quantum Computing.
